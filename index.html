🎵 Speaking Tips

1. Speak with Conviction: Say each affirmation aloud with confidence and belief.

2. Look in the Mirror: Make eye contact with yourself while declaring these truths.

3. Repeat Daily: Consistency builds faith and transforms your thought patterns.

4. Add Personal Touch: Insert your name: "I, [Your Name], am fearfully and wonderfully made."

5. Pray First: Ask God to help you believe and receive these truths about who you are.

📱 App Guide

What this app does:
Identity in Christ helps you overcome shame and self-doubt through powerful Scripture-based affirmations about your true identity in God.
How to use:
• Start each day with "Today's Truth"
• Explore affirmations by category (Worth, Strength, Love)
• Speak affirmations aloud for maximum impact
• Access crisis support for immediate encouragement
• Track your progress and build consistency
Install on your phone:
📱 iPhone: Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
📱 Android: Tap Menu → Add to Home Screen
Best Practices:
• Use daily for 21 days to build new thought patterns
• Speak affirmations aloud when possible
• Look in a mirror while declaring these truths
• Start and end your day with identity affirmations
This app works offline and saves your progress locally on your device.
